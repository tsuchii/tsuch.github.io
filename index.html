Hello! I'm Scott.

I’m the Director of Product Design at GoNoodle, where we use movement, mindfulness, and dance parties to help kids be their best selves. I enjoy learning about people through conversation and observation, and using that knowledge to build products that fit neatly into the day-to-day lives of kids, parents, and teachers.

Some friends and I organize Nashville UX, supporting a group of amazing user experience professionals here in Nashville. We host monthly meetups, encourage lively discussion and debate on Slack, and do whatever else we can to promote a thriving user experience community in the Music City.

In my spare time, I co-host DadJokes, a podcast where my friends Joey and Garrett help me through the early stages of fatherhood. I love Motown, I'll always make room for dessert, and I firmly believe that Han shot first.
